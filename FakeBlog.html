Developing Skills Over 20 Years of Painting

Over 20 years ago I painted my first figurines. I loved the process and was entranced at so many options. It was a fun hobby I initially got into with some friends and it turned into a passion I have refined to this day. The lessons I learned about patience, attention to detail and how to work with my hands have help me thoughout my life. Now I want to pass that knowledge on to you so you can improve your minis.

Though its easy to see everything all at once now after years of work, it took a long time to develop my skills and amass the large amount of miniatures I have today. Lots of saving, painting and comissioned work gave me even more experience and it helped me build out my hobby until it was a full time business!
